# Public FAQ

## Is DriveMind open source?
DriveMind is publicly visible, but not freely usable for production or commercial purposes without permission.

## Is it a prompt pack?
No. DriveMind is intended as a safety-and-reliability layer, not a one-off prompt trick.

## Is it a memory tool?
Not only. Memory is one part, but DriveMind also focuses on persistence, review, and judgment.

## Who is it for?
Developers, advanced AI users, and builders who want agents to behave more reliably in real work.

## Why not just make the agent try harder?
Because persistence without judgment becomes recklessness. DriveMind is about bounded persistence plus safe escalation.
