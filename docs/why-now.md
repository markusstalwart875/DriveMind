# Why DriveMind, Why Now

The next bottleneck for AI agents is no longer only intelligence.
It is reliability in real work.

As agents begin to handle longer tasks, recurring workflows, and operational work, four practical gaps become obvious:
- they forget too much,
- they stop too early,
- they wait too passively,
- they make poor tradeoffs under uncertainty.

These are not niche problems.
They are common for anyone seriously trying to use agents beyond one-shot chat.

That is why DriveMind matters now.
It addresses the shift from:
- "Can an agent answer?"
- to "Can an agent be trusted to keep working well?"
