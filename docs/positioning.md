# DriveMind Positioning

## One-line
DriveMind is a safety-and-reliability layer for AI agents.

## Expanded
DriveMind is designed for builders who no longer struggle with model intelligence alone, but with agent reliability in real work.

It addresses a practical gap:
- agents can respond,
- but they often do not persist correctly,
- do not preserve useful lessons,
- and do not make safe tradeoffs under uncertainty.

DriveMind exists to close that gap.

## Why it matters
The next bottleneck for agents is not only smarter answers.
It is safer, steadier, more reusable execution.
