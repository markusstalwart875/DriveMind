# DriveMind Design Principles

## 1. Reliability over cleverness
The goal is not to sound smarter. The goal is to behave more reliably in real work.

## 2. Persistence with boundaries
Do not give up too early. But do not push blindly past safety or uncertainty boundaries.

## 3. Memory must be reusable
Logging is not enough. What matters is turning experience into future leverage.

## 4. Reflection must change behavior
A review is useful only if it modifies future execution.

## 5. Human authority stays explicit
The human owns goals, boundaries, and high-risk decisions. DriveMind improves execution within those constraints.
