# DriveMind Usage Notice

DriveMind is publicly visible for research, learning, evaluation, and discussion.

## Allowed
- reading the source and documents
- studying the design and ideas
- internal evaluation and experimentation
- discussion, citation, and academic/reference use

## Not allowed without prior permission
- production deployment
- redistribution
- derivative commercial use
- repackaging as a product or service
- selling, sublicensing, or embedding into commercial offerings

## Contact requirement
If you want to use DriveMind in production, integrate it into a product, redistribute it, or build commercial work on top of it, please contact the authors first.

## Rationale
DriveMind is being developed as a controlled open project. Public visibility is intended to share ideas and direction, while actual use remains permission-based.
