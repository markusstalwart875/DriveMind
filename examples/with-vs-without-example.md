# With vs Without Example

## Task
Investigate and fix a system issue that looks simple at first but may hide deeper causes.

## Without DriveMind
- the agent identifies the surface symptom
- proposes a likely explanation
- tries one action
- stops early when uncertainty increases
- ends without preserving a useful lesson

## With DriveMind
- the agent restates the objective and success criteria
- checks the first direct safe path
- gathers more evidence when blocked
- tries a bounded alternative instead of repeating blindly
- escalates when authority or risk becomes unclear
- writes a short post-task review and reusable lesson

## Difference
Without DriveMind, the task may end as a one-time attempt.
With DriveMind, the task becomes a reusable learning event even if full success is not immediate.
